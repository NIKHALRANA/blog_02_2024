Robot That Watched Surgical Videos Now Operates With Human-Level Skill

https://scitechdaily.com/robot-that-watched-surgical-videos-now-operates-with-human-level-skill/ November 13, 2024

Researchers from Johns Hopkins and Stanford Universities have advanced autonomous robotic surgery using imitation learning, enabling a robot to learn surgical techniques from a large collection of recorded procedures rather than being programmed step-by-step. This approach holds promise for reducing medical errors and improving surgical precision, marking a significant step forward in autonomous robotic surgeries.

Colonizing Mars: The Simple Device That Could Turn CO2 Into Vital Fuel

https://scitechdaily.com/colonizing-mars-the-simple-device-that-could-turn-co2-into-vital-fuel/ November 10, 2024

Chemists at the University of British Columbia (UBC) demonstrated a new way to convert CO₂ into useful fuels and chemicals using thermoelectric generators that operate with modest temperature differences. This technology could be powered by temperature differentials found in environments ranging from Earth's geothermal installations to Mars.

How AI Is Turning DNA Secrets Into Lifesaving Medical Insights

https://scitechdaily.com/how-ai-is-turning-dna-secrets-into-lifesaving-medical-insights/ November 17, 2024

AI is revolutionizing genomics by decoding DNA’s complexities and transforming genetic data into lifesaving medical insights. Advanced tools like CODA and GROVER leverage machine learning to design DNA sequences that precisely control gene expression, enabling targeted therapies for diseases like Parkinson’s and HIV.
