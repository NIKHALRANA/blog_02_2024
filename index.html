Emerging Technologies

A comprehensive study of technological change

https://news.mit.edu/2021/comprehensive-study-technological-change-0802 August 2, 2021

The societal impacts of rapid technological change are evident across numerous domains, including healthcare (mRNA vaccines), industry (automation), transportation (drones), and environmental management (climate change technologies). The speed at which these technologies advance can significantly influence their societal effects. Rapid improvement in a technology's performance can serve as a strong signal of its potential importance and transformative power.
